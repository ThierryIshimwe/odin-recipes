Apple Fruits

Description

Ripe apples are like nature's candies, shiny and colorful. They taste both sweet and a little tangy, creating a delicious mix. When you bite into one, it's juicy and crunchy.

They smell really good, like the best part of a cozy fall day. Ripe apples are just perfect, a tasty treat from the orchard.

Nutrients in Ripe Apples:

  • Vitamins and minerals
  • Dietary Fibers
  • Antioxidants

Steps to Prepare Ripe Apple Fruits:

  1. Wash the apple fruits under running water.
  2. Peel the apples or leave the skin on for added nutrition.
  3. Core the apples by removing the seeds and stems.
  4. Slice the apples into thin or thick slices.
  5. Place the apple slices in a bowl.
